L'Anconetano

A restaurant specializing in traditional cuisine from Ancona, a city in the Marche region of Italy.

Where is L'Anconetano located?

L'Anconetano is located in the heart of Plovdiv.

"Il fritto di pesce"

It is a fried seafood dish featuring a variety of fish such as shrimp, squid, and other local catches.

"I moscioli all'Anconetana"

"I Moscioli all'Anconetana" is a traditional seafood dish native to Ancona, featuring mussels (мидите) as the main ingredient. It is often prepared with tomato sauce, lemon, and onions to create a rich and flavorful culinary experience.

"La Merenda Anconetana"

Another example of a traditional pizza commonly found in Ancona is distinguished by its unique texture and large size. Unlike typical pizzas, it is usually sold by the slice rather than as a whole pie.

History of "L'Anconetana"
